## Question 10

**Q:** For the track optimization step (deduplication based on significant deviation), what thresholds or rules should be used? For example: minimum distance change in meters, minimum heading change in degrees, ROT threshold, minimum time gap â€” provide numeric defaults or say "tunable by config" if you prefer to make them adjustable.

**A:** Filter. For **new** it checks 2 previous recodrs, **prev** and **prev-prev**. **new** record is always added. The filter if removes, it removes **prev** record. Conditions are as follows. Shall be applied in the order as follows.
 - dt between prev and prev-prev is less than threshold (adjustable as an edit field in "Thresholds" panel in edit box), the prev record is discarded.
	- use prev-prev record, new record, calculate by linear interpolation (time as an argument) the **interpolated-prev** record at the time of prev record time. If distance between positions of prev and interpolated-prev records is less than a threshold (distance tolerance field in Thresholds pannel), discard prev record.
